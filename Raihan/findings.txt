dutta et al (2005) Amit and Shoemaker (1993) : capability : relative efficiency in transforming input -> output

firm with higher level of output may not be more capable than firms with low level of output.
Rather firm with high capability converts input -> output more efficiently. generating greater output.
This relative trasformative efficiency is essential to survive
